Aging in Netherlands

According to the CBS, the absolute death rate from dementia has increased within the Netherlands. It also emerged that the lowest mortality rate from dementia is in Zeeland and North and East Gelderland.

This regional spread has been tested against the national average, where the calculated the total death and death by cause or death by region.

The number of people with dementia will rise explosively in the future to more than half a million in 2040 as a result of the aging population. Until 2050, the number of people with dementia will increase further to more than 620,000.
