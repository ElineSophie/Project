Types of dementia

Dementia is the name for a combination of symptoms, where the brain can no longer process information properly. Dementia is a collective name for more than fifty diseases. The most common form of dementia is Alzheimer's disease. In addition, vascular dementia, frontotemporal dementia (FTD) and Lewy body dementia are common.
