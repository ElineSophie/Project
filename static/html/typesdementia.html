Types of dementia

Dementia is the name for a combination of symptoms, where the brain can no longer process information properly. Dementia is a collective name for more than fifty diseases. The most common types of dementia are Alzheimer's, vascular dementia, frontotemporal dementia and Lewy body dementia.

This pie chart shows the prevalence of the most common types of dementia.

- Dementia not specified (can be a mix of different types of dementia)
- Alzheimer disease + Vascular dementia (AD + VaD)
- Vascular dementia (VaD)
- Parkinson's disease dementia (PDD)
- Frontotemporal dementia (FTD)
- Dementia with Lewy Body's (DLB)
- Other
